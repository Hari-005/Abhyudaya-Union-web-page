About us

The heartbeat of campus life.

Founded with the spirit of Abhyudaya, meaning progress and prosperity, our union exists to amplify every student's voice. From academic support to cultural celebration, we build a campus that uplifts.

We work hand-in-hand with college administration, industry leaders, and alumni to create meaningful opportunities for the students of today and the changemakers of tomorrow.
