# Abhyudaya College Union Website

A multi-page static website for Abhyudaya College Union, Government Engineering College Kozhikode.

## Pages

- `index.html` - home page with animated hero cards and notice highlights
- `members.html` - union office bearer details
- `events.html` - events, notices, and schedule cards
- `report.html` - union report photo slider using images `1.jpeg` through `11.jpeg`
- `hostels.html` - nearby hostel/PG details with photos
- `contact.html` - union desk contact details and static request form

## Design Notes

- Typography: Syne and DM Sans through Google Fonts
- Palette: saffron, indigo, emerald, cream, and dark charcoal
- Layout: alternating full-bleed dark sections and cream sections
- Motion: CSS-only floating cards in the hero
- Assets: generated local images and numbered union report photos saved under `assets/images`
