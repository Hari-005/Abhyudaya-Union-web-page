Academic departments

Seven departments, one campus voice.

A quick student-friendly guide to the departments represented across Abhyudaya College Union and Government Engineering College Kozhikode.

Applied Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering

Blends electronics, sensors, measurement, automation, and control systems for process industries, labs, and smart devices.

Civil Engineering

Focuses on planning, designing, and maintaining the built environment, from structures and transportation systems to water resources and sustainable infrastructure.

Computer Science and Design

Combines computing, software development, design thinking, interface design, and creative technology for human-centered digital products.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Connects hardware, embedded systems, signal processing, communication networks, and digital technologies for the connected world.

Chemical Engineering

Studies processes that transform raw materials into useful products through chemistry, design, safety, and sustainable production methods.

Mechanical Engineering

Builds strong foundations in machines, thermal systems, manufacturing, design, and practical problem-solving for industry and innovation.

Electrical and Computer Engineering

Covers circuits, power systems, control, computing fundamentals, and intelligent electrical technologies for modern infrastructure.

Representation

Every department deserves a clear route to the union desk.

Department representatives help students communicate academic concerns, event needs, welfare requests, and campus suggestions to the union team.
